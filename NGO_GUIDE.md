# 🌉 EduBridge — Guide for NGOs and Schools

Welcome! This guide helps you use EduBridge with your students.
**No technical knowledge required.**

---

## What is EduBridge?

EduBridge is a free AI tutor that helps children learn **Math, Science, and English**.
It is available 24/7, completely free, and works on any phone, tablet, or computer.

---

## How to Use with Students

### Step 1: Open EduBridge
Visit **https://<your-app>.vercel.app** (works on phone, tablet, or computer).

### Step 2: Choose a Subject
Help the student pick **Math, Science, or English**.

### Step 3: Choose an Age Level
- 🐣 **Little Kids** (6–10 years)
- 🦋 **Older Kids** (11–14 years)

### Step 4: Start Asking!
The student types or speaks their question, and EduBridge explains it simply.

---

## ✨ Fun Features for Kids

EduBridge is built to be friendly and easy for young learners:

- **🔊 Listen button** — every answer can be read aloud. Perfect for children who are still
  learning to read, or who learn better by listening.
- **🎤 Voice questions** — children can tap the microphone and *speak* their question instead
  of typing it. Great for younger kids.
- **✨ Example questions** — when a child opens a subject, EduBridge suggests questions they can
  tap, so they're never stuck staring at a blank screen.
- **⭐ Stars** — children earn a star for every question they ask, which gently encourages
  curiosity and keeps learning playful.
- **తెలుగు / English toggle** — switch the language any time for students who are more
  comfortable in Telugu.

---

## Tips for Teachers

- ✅ Let students ask in their own words.
- ✅ Encourage follow-up questions — they can keep asking "Why?" to go deeper.
- ✅ Use the **Listen** button for early readers.
- ✅ Use the **Telugu** toggle for students who struggle with English.
- ✅ Use the **microphone** for students who find typing hard.
- ✅ Use it as a homework helper after school.

---

## What EduBridge Can Help With

- **Math:** addition, subtraction, multiplication, fractions, shapes, basic algebra
- **Science:** animals, plants, weather, the body, space, simple experiments
- **English:** reading, writing, grammar, spelling, vocabulary

---

## What EduBridge Cannot Do

- ❌ Replace a real teacher
- ❌ Give exam answers directly
- ❌ Access the internet
- ❌ Remember previous conversations

---

## Seeing How Much It's Being Used

On the welcome screen, scroll to the bottom and tap **"📊 For teachers: view usage stats."**
You'll see how many questions have been asked, the most popular subjects, and daily activity.
